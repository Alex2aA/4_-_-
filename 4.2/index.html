Renewable Energy Surpasses Coal in U.S. Power Generation

For the first time in history, renewable energy sources have surpassed coal in electricity generation in the United States. This milestone reflects a significant shift towards cleaner energy solutions, with wind and solar power contributing to over 23% of the nation's electricity supply in the last quarter. Experts predict this trend will continue as more states adopt green energy policies.
